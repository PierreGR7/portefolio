This is the first entry in my **Articles** section. Unlike my [projects](/#projects) — which are hands-on, code-driven builds — this space is for **opinions, takes and news** on data engineering, AI and the broader tech world: the kind of posts I'd share on Medium or LinkedIn.

## What to expect here

- Short, opinionated takes on tools and trends I actually use.
- Lessons learned from real pipelines, dashboards and data architectures.
- Commentary on news that matters to data practitioners.

## How this is built

Each article is a simple Markdown file in `content/articles/`. To publish a new one, just duplicate this file, change the front-matter (`title`, `description`, `date`, `image`) and write your content below. It will automatically show up in the Articles section on the homepage.

> Feel free to edit or delete this starter post — it's only here to show the layout.
